Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to see SEO results?

While initial improvements can often be seen within 1-3 months (like better indexing or keyword visibility), significant results such as substantial traffic growth typically manifest within 4-6 months, sometimes longer depending on competition and website history. SEO is a long-term strategy focused on effective growth.

What makes your approach different?

My approach is built on transparency, data-driven decisions, and a personalized strategy tailored to your specific business goals. I combine technical expertise with strategic content and social media management, focusing on measurable outcomes and clear communication. My background in QA ensures meticulous execution.

Do you guarantee first-page rankings?

No ethical SEO specialist can guarantee specific rankings (especially #1) because Google's algorithm is complex and constantly changing. However, I focus on proven strategies and best practices to significantly improve your search visibility, target relevant keywords, and drive qualified traffic, which leads to higher rankings over time.

What's included in your services?

My services are typically project-based or retainer-based and customized. Common components include technical SEO audits, keyword research, comprehensive SEO optimization, content strategy/creation, social media management, WordPress services, link building analysis, cold emailing, directory setup, GEO, regular performance monitoring, and detailed reporting. We'll define the exact scope based on your needs.

How do you measure success?

Success is measured against the goals we establish together. Key performance indicators (KPIs) typically include improvements in organic keyword rankings, growth in organic website traffic, increases in qualified leads or conversions, enhanced social media engagement, and improvements in technical site health. I provide regular reports detailing progress on these metrics.
